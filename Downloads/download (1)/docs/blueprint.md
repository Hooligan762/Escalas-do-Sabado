# **App Name**: Saturday Schedule Scale

## Core Features:

- Technician Roster: Manage a roster of technicians: Ismael, Batista, Geraldo, and Bernardo.
- Saturday Shift Calendar: Display a calendar view of Saturday shifts from 2025 onwards.
- Automated Shift Scheduling: Generative AI suggests optimized shift schedules based on technician availability and rules.
- Shift Swap System: Tool to request and manage shift swaps between technicians, ensuring fairness and adherence to swap rules. The LLM reasons whether or not to accept a shift swap request based on who is offering and who is accepting the swap, using the 'rule of saturday'
- Schedule Conflict Detection: Alerts to detect scheduling conflicts and rule violations before they occur.
- Reporting and Analytics: Generate reports on shift coverage and technician workload.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#3F51B5) to convey professionalism and trustworthiness.
- Background color: Light gray (#F0F0F3) for a clean, modern backdrop.
- Accent color: Soft orange (#FFAB40) for interactive elements.
- Headline font: 'Poppins' sans-serif for headlines
- Body font: 'PT Sans' sans-serif for body
- Use clear and intuitive icons to represent different functions.
- Maintain a clean and organized layout for easy navigation.
- Use subtle animations to indicate changes.
